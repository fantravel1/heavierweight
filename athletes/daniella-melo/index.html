Training Philosophy

  • Technique-first approach on every rep
  • High frequency training with autoregulation
  • Emphasis on video review and self-correction
  • Long-term progression over short-term PRs

Advice for Lifters

"Don't chase numbers before you chase positions. Every lifter I see plateau is trying to add weight before they've mastered the movement. Slow down, get the technique right, and the strength will come."

"And be patient. This sport rewards consistency over years, not weeks. Show up, do the work, trust the process."
