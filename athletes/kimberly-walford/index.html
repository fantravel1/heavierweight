A Legend of the Sport

Kimberly Walford's career spans more than 15 years of international competition. She's represented Team USA at more World Championships than almost any other lifter, earning multiple gold medals and setting numerous American records along the way.

Her longevity in the sport is remarkable. While many athletes peak and fade, Kim has remained competitive at the highest level for over a decade, adapting her training and approach as her body and the sport have evolved.

"I've been doing this a long time. What keeps me coming back is the challenge‚Äîthere's always something to improve, always a way to get better. The day I stop learning is the day I retire."

Trailblazer

Kim has been a visible presence in American powerlifting for years, representing the sport at its highest levels. She's helped pave the way for the current generation of female lifters by showing that women's powerlifting deserves recognition and respect.

Beyond her competition achievements, she's known for being approachable, supportive of newer lifters, and a positive ambassador for the sport.

The Deadlift
