## Partnership Terms Framework

### Content Contributor Agreements

**Standard Terms:**
- Byline attribution for all published content
- Editorial control remains with HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM
- Contributor retains right to republish after 6 months
- Payment negotiable based on content type and reach

**Content Types:**
| Type | Est. Compensation | Requirements |
|------|------------------|--------------|
| Guest Article | $200-500 | 1,500+ words, original |
| Expert Quote | $0-50 | Brief contribution |
| Interview Feature | $100-300 | 30-60 min interview |
| Ongoing Column | Negotiable | Monthly commitment |

### Affiliate Partnerships

**Standard Terms:**
- Honest reviews (positive, negative, or mixed)
- Disclosure in all affiliate content
- Commission rates: 5-15% depending on brand
- Cookie duration: 30-90 days

### Sponsored Content

**Standard Terms:**
- Clearly labeled as sponsored/partnership
- Editorial control for accuracy and tone
- Alignment with HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM values required
- Minimum 6-month exclusivity typical
