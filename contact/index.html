How do I submit a question for an article?

Email us at hello@heavierweight.com with your question. We can't promise to answer everything, but we read every message and use common questions to inform future content.

Can I request a specific topic?

Absolutely! We love hearing what our readers want to learn about. Send topic suggestions to hello@heavierweight.com.
