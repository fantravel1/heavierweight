Body Positivity Advocate

Emily has been vocal about body image in sport. As a super-heavyweight lifter, she's faced comments and assumptions her entire career. Rather than shrink from this, she's used her platform to advocate for body positivity and self-acceptance.

"My body is strong. It can do incredible things. I'm not going to apologize for taking up space. I'm going to celebrate what this body can achieve."

Her message resonates far beyond weightlifting. She's become a role model for athletes of all sizes, showing that strength comes in every shape and that success doesn't require conforming to narrow body standards.
