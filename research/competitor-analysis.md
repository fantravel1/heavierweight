## Detailed Competitor Breakdown

### BarBend

**Overview:** Largest strength sports media site. Covers powerlifting, weightlifting, strongman, CrossFit, bodybuilding.

**Strengths:**
- Massive authority and traffic
- Comprehensive competition coverage
- Strong affiliate revenue (equipment reviews)
- Large content library

**Weaknesses:**
- Generic approach to women's content
- Often rehashed or superficial women's articles
- "Add 'for women' to headline" approach
- No female-specific expertise or voice

**Content Gap Opportunity:**
BarBend publishes ~5-10 women-specific articles per month, mostly generic ("Best exercises for women"). Deep technical content for female lifters is minimal.

---

### Girls Gone Strong

**Overview:** Women's fitness education and certification platform.

**Strengths:**
- Strong women-focused brand
- Quality educational content
- Expert contributor network
- Pregnancy/postpartum expertise

**Weaknesses:**
- Content gated behind certifications
- Focus on coaches, not lifters
- Broader fitness focus (not competition-specific)
- Limited free content depth

**Content Gap Opportunity:**
GGS targets fitness professionals, not end consumers. Free content is limited. HEAVIERWEIGHT serves the athlete/lifter directly.

---

### Stronger By Science

**Overview:** Evidence-based training content for serious lifters.

**Strengths:**
- Exceptional research depth
- Strong credibility
- MASS research review subscription
- Free programs and resources

**Weaknesses:**
- Not women-specific (male default)
- Academic writing style
- Limited practical/actionable content
- No equipment/gear content

**Content Gap Opportunity:**
SBS rarely addresses female-specific considerations. HEAVIERWEIGHT applies same research rigor through a female lens.

---

### Stronger By The Day (Meg Squats)

**Overview:** App-based programming platform with community focus.

**Strengths:**
- Strong female powerlifting community
- Personality-driven brand
- Quality programming
- Active engagement

**Weaknesses:**
- Personality-dependent
- Content tied to paid app
- Limited free content library
- Competition (singlet/federation) content minimal

**Content Gap Opportunity:**
SBTD is programming-focused. HEAVIERWEIGHT covers competition, equipment, science, and lifestyle comprehensively.
