Beyond the Platform

Stefi Cohen isn't just a powerlifter‚Äîshe's a Doctor of Physical Therapy, a former professional soccer player, and a successful entrepreneur. She holds a DPT from the University of Miami and uses her education to inform both her training and her coaching of other athletes.

Born in Venezuela, Stefi moved to the United States for college on a soccer scholarship. When injuries ended her soccer career, she discovered powerlifting‚Äîand proceeded to break every record in sight.

"I don't just want to be strong. I want to understand WHY I'm strong. The science behind the lifting is what fascinates me."

Training Philosophy

Stefi's approach combines high frequency, intelligent autoregulation, and a focus on addressing weaknesses. Key principles:

  • High frequency training (squatting and deadlifting multiple times per week)
  • RPE-based programming to manage fatigue
  • Heavy emphasis on posterior chain development
  • Treating weaknesses as opportunities
  • Taking recovery as seriously as training

Hybrid Athlete

Stefi has competed in multiple sports and formats: powerlifting (multiple federations), strongman, and even boxing. She's proof that being a specialist doesn't mean being one-dimensional.

Her willingness to step outside powerlifting has made her a better athlete overall‚Äîand shows that the skills built through strength training transfer broadly.

Advice for Aspiring Lifters
