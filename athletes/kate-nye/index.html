Mental Strength

Kate has been open about her mental health journey, including managing bipolar disorder. Her willingness to discuss these challenges has made her a role model beyond just her athletic achievements.

"Being an elite athlete and managing mental health isn't easy," she's said. "But it's possible. Both require daily work, daily attention. Neither defines all of who I am."

This openness has helped destigmatize mental health discussions in strength sports and shown athletes that seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
