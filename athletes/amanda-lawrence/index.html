The Squat Queen

Amanda Lawrence is known for one thing above all else: her squat. She's squatted weights that seemed impossible in the 84kg class, with a level of depth and control that makes every lift look effortless.

But don't let her squat overshadow the rest of her lifting. Her bench press and deadlift are also world-class, making her one of the most complete lifters in the sport.

"I love squatting. It's where I feel most confident, most powerful. When the bar is on my back, everything else fades away."

Rise to the Top

Amanda burst onto the powerlifting scene as a teenager and has been breaking records ever since. Her rapid rise has made her one of the most-watched lifters in the sport, with meets regularly livestreamed to thousands of viewers.

What sets her apart isn't just raw strength‚Äîit's consistency. She rarely misses lifts in competition, a testament to her technical mastery and competition preparation.

Training Approach

  • High volume squatting with variations
  • Emphasis on positioning and technique
  • Strategic use of pauses and tempo work
  • Works with coach Matt Gary and team NYBBS

Advice for Young Lifters

"Don't rush. I know everyone wants to be strong immediately, but the athletes who last are the ones who build carefully. Take time to learn technique. Take time to recover. The records will come."
