# Contributing to HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM

Thank you for your interest in contributing to HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM's content strategy.

## Content Philosophy

HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM is committed to:

- **Evidence-based content**: All claims backed by research or expert opinion
- **Inclusive representation**: Featuring diverse body types, ages, and skill levels
- **Women-specific focus**: Content created for female lifters, not adapted from general sources
- **Accessible expertise**: Research-quality information in readable format

## Contributor Types

### Expert Contributors

We welcome contributions from:

- **Coaches**: Experienced strength coaches with female clients
- **Researchers**: Sports scientists with relevant expertise
- **Specialists**: Pelvic floor PTs, registered dietitians, sports psychologists
- **Athletes**: Current or former competitive lifters

### Content Requirements

All contributions must:

1. Align with our [Style Guide](./editorial/style-guide.md)
2. Include proper citations for research claims
3. Use inclusive, non-condescending language
4. Provide actionable, practical information

## Submission Process

### For Article Ideas

1. Review existing content in the [pillars](./pillars/) directory
2. Check the [editorial calendar](./editorial/) for planned content
3. Submit article proposals including:
   - Proposed title
   - Primary keyword target
   - Brief outline (3-5 main points)
   - Your relevant expertise/credentials

### For Corrections

If you find factual errors or outdated information:

1. Open an issue with:
   - Specific article reference
   - Incorrect information identified
   - Suggested correction with source

### For Athlete Profiles

If you're an athlete or represent one:

1. Review our [profile format](./pillars/02-compete.md#profile-article-format)
2. Submit:
   - Competition history and achievements
   - Availability for interview
   - Social media handles
   - High-resolution action photos (if available)

## Partnership Inquiries

For formal partnership discussions, including:

- Regular contributor arrangements
- Expert review roles
- Sponsored content opportunities
- Brand collaborations

Please reference our [Partnership Report](./partners.md) for priority categories.

## Code of Conduct

All contributors must:

- Respect the experiences of all body types and skill levels
- Avoid language that shames, judges, or excludes
- Prioritize accuracy over sensationalism
- Disclose any conflicts of interest

## Rights and Attribution

By contributing, you agree that:

- HEAVIERWEIGHT.COM retains editorial control over all published content
- Your contribution becomes part of our proprietary content library
- You will receive appropriate byline attribution
- You warrant that your contribution is original and factually accurate

## Questions?

Open an issue for general questions about contributing.
